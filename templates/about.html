About SmartCredit

SmartCredit is a credit card decision support system designed to assist banking institutions in evaluating applications more efficiently and consistently, with a focus on the Nepalese banking sector.

The system analyzes applicant financial and demographic information to generate eligibility insights, supporting bank officers in making timely and informed credit approval decisions while retaining full administrative control.
